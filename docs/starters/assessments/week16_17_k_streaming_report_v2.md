## Key Factors for Kicker Success

### 1. Vegas Lines (Most Important)
- High implied team totals (24+) = more scoring opportunities
- High O/U totals (47+) = shootout potential
- Favorites score more than underdogs

### 2. Venue & Weather
- **Dome games** = No weather variables, consistent conditions
- **Cold** (<40Â°F) = Reduced accuracy on 45+ yard attempts
- **Wind** (15+ mph) = Avoid entirely

### 3. Offensive Efficiency
- **High red zone TD%** = More XPs, fewer FG attempts
- **Low red zone TD%** = More FG opportunities
- Better offenses = more scoring trips overall

### 4. Opponent Defense
- **Strong defenses** = Teams stall in red zone = More FGs
- **Weak defenses** = Easy TDs or quick punts
