## Plan Comparison

| Factor | Plan A (Stream QB + DST) | Plan B (Stream DST Only) |
|--------|--------------------------|--------------------------|
| Week 14 QB | Lawrence vs IND (~17-22 pts) | Mahomes vs HOU (~14-16 pts) |
| Week 14 QB Edge | **+3-6 points** | â€” |
| Roster Flexibility | 1 open spot | 1 open spot |
| RB Depth | 5 RBs (incl Tracy) | 5 RBs (incl Tracy) |
| Risk | Lawrence boom/bust | Mahomes floor is safe |
| Complexity | 4 transactions | 3 transactions |

### Recommendation

**Plan A is higher upside** - The Texans are genuinely elite vs QBs (#1, 12.5 PPG allowed). Lawrence vs IND's 29th-ranked pass D is a significant upgrade. The extra transaction is worth the ~3-6 projected point gain.

**Plan B is safer** - If you trust Mahomes to overcome any matchup, or don't want to roster a one-week QB rental, this is simpler. You still get Strange + streaming DST + Hampton activation.
