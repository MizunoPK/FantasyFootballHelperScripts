This is called a Start 7 Fantasy League				
You will be drafting 14 players and a defense				
of those you will be using 7 players, a kicker and a defense each week				
Your 7 players starting each week will be arranged like so:				
				
1 Quarterback				
2 Running Backs				
2 Wide Recievers 		<- these are the 7 starters		
1 Tight end				
1 Wide Reciever or Running back				
				
1 Kicker				
and 1 Defense				
				
Any player not starting for your team in a given week will be on your bench or in your reserve				
you have 6 bench slots (these are your backups)				
				
and 3 reserve slots for injured players				
				


SCORING:			
Passing Yards:	1 point per 25 yards					
Passing Touchdowns:	4 points					
Interceptions Thrown:	-2 points					
Rushing Yards:	1 point per 10 yards					
Rushing Touchdowns:	6 points					
Receptions:	1 point					
Receiving Yards:	1 point per 10 yards					
Receiving Touchdowns:	6 points					
Kickoff and Punt Return Touchdowns:	6 points					
Fumble Recovered for TD:	6 points					
Fumbles Lost:	-2 points					
2-Point Conversions:	2 points					
						
Your starting players and defense will earn you points through the outcomes of their weekly games						
you will be battling someone else in the league to earn more points than they did that week						
At the end of the season the 6 teams with the best record will advance to the playoffs to determine the winner
